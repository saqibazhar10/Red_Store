RedStore!

RedStore Pvt Ltd. is an online marketplace and logistics company which operates in markets of South Asia and Southeast Asia. It was founded in 2012 as an online fashion e-commerce marketplace in Pakistan by Muneeb Maayr (Founder) & Farees Shah (Co-Founder) at the based venture builder, Rocket Internet. Daraz Group operates e-commerce platforms and logistics service in Bangladesh, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka.[1] Bjarke Mikkelsen is the current CEO of Daraz.[2][3] In May 2018, Daraz Group was acquired by the Chinese e-commerce company Alibaba Group from Rocket Internet.
